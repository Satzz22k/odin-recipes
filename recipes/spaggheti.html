Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with spaghetti topped with a rich meat-based tomato sauce. It is savory, slightly tangy, and very comforting.


Spaghetti Bolognese Ingredients

  • 100g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1/2 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Dried oregano (optional)

*Images is for illustration purpose only


Tools

  • Pot
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula or wooden spoon
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Strainer

How To Cooks (Steps)

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  6. Drain the spaghetti and serve with the sauce on top.

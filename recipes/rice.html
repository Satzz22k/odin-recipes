Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a stir-fried rice dish cooked with ingredients like garlic, chili, vegetables, eggs, and sometimes meat or seafood. It has a savory flavor and a slightly smoky aroma.


In Indonesia, it is called Nasi Goreng, which literally means “fried rice.” Nasi Goreng is one of Indonesia’s most popular dishes and is often served with a fried egg and crackers on the side.


Fried Rice Ingredients

  • 1 plate of cooked white rice (preferably cold rice)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 small shallot (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • A small amount of chopped green onion
  • Crackers (optional)

*Image is for illustration purpose only

Tools

  • Frying pan or wok
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Stove

How To Cook (Steps)

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the garlic and shallot until fragrant.
  3. Crack the egg into the pan and scramble until fully cooked.
  4. Add the rice and mix well.
  5. Add sweet soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir until evenly combined and heated through.
  6. Add green onion, stir briefly, then remove from heat.
  7. Serve hot, optionally with crackers on the side.
